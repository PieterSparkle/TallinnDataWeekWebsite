About the Event

Tallinn Data Week is a unique gathering focused on the human side of data work. We believe that when professionals thrive personally, organizational success naturally follows. Join us for a week of inspiration, learning, and connection.

What to Expect

A week-long celebration of data professionals featuring:

  • Opening ceremony on Monday
  • Themed events throughout the week
  • Special closing event on Friday
  • Networking opportunities
  • Actionable strategies for career growth
  • Analytics innovation insights

Event Schedule

Each day features expert speakers, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities tailored to the day's focus area. Detailed agenda will be announced closer to the event date.

Venue

Tallinn Data Week 2025 will take place at the T1 conference venue in Tallinn, Estonia.

T1 Conference Venue

Peterburi tee 2, Tallinn

The T1 conference venue offers modern facilities and is conveniently located in Tallinn with excellent public transportation connections.

Getting There

  • By Public Transport: Tram lines 2 and 4 stop at Ülemiste, a short walk from the venue.
  • By Car: Ample parking is available at the T1 shopping center.
  • From Airport: T1 is just a 5-minute drive from Tallinn Airport.
