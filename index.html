About the Event

Tallinn Data Week is a unique gathering focused on the human side of data work. We believe that when professionals thrive personally, organizational success naturally follows. Join us for a week of inspiration, learning, and connection!

What to Expect

A week-long celebration of data professionals featuring:

  • Opening day on Monday - it's a conference with a few hundred people
  • Themed events throughout the week - three smaller events every day, read on!
  • Special closing event on Friday
  • Networking opportunities
  • Actionable strategies for career growth
  • Analytics innovation insights

This is for you if you...

  • work with data in private sector, public sector, academy, or just for fun
  • have a job title that contains 'data'
  • use data in decision-making
  • lead people who work with data
  • are curious about what the people do who work with data

PS. It's also cool to send this to someone you know who would find it interesting :)

How to attend

The format has two different event types. On Monday is a regular conference that you should attend. It's free.

Tuesday through Friday there are themed events, three hours each, starting at 9am, 1pm and 5pm. Each will be in different place, with various topics and speakers. These are organized into focus areas per day (below). Pick and choose the ones you wish. Or all of them :) All these events are also free.

On Friday evening there will be a closing event, in a pub! You probably want to attend that one. Yes, this one is also free to enter, the rest is up to you.
